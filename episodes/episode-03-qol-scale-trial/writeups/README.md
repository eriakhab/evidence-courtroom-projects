## Purpose

The writeups serve three primary functions:

1. Document analytical decisions and modeling strategy
2. Interpret statistical results in applied context
3. Communicate implications for patients, clinicians, and researchers

These materials bridge formal psychometric analysis and accessible
evidence-based storytelling.

## Writing Standards

All materials in this folder adhere to the following principles:

- Transparency in methods and assumptions
- Clear linkage between data, analysis, and conclusions
- Explicit discussion of uncertainty and limitations
- Avoidance of overstated claims

Statistical terminology is used precisely and explained where necessary.
