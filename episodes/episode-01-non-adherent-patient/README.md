## Folder Structure

### `writeups/`
Manuscripts, technical reports, and episode briefs.

### `analysis/`
Statistical code, modeling workflows, and diagnostics.

---

## Research Focus

Key analytical themes include:

- Medication adherence behavior  
- Health outcomes and utilization  
- Measurement and psychometrics  
- Regression and predictive modeling  
- Bias and confounding assessment  

---

## Reproducibility

All analyses are designed to be reproducible using
the scripts provided in `analysis/`, subject to
data availability and licensing restrictions.
